: rng chocolate chip cookies

ingredients:
  • 1 c softened butter
  • ½ c white sugar
  • ½ c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla ex.
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2½ c all-purpose flour
  • 2 c chocolate chips
steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Cream together brown sugar, white sugar, and butter
  3. Add eggs and vanilla
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually stir into creamed mixture.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Using a scoop or rounded spoon, drop onto cookie sheet.
  7. Bake 8-10 minutes until light brown.

: vanishing oatmeal cookies

ingredients:
  • ½ c + 6 Tbsp softened butter
  • ¾ c firmly packed brown sugar
  • ½ c granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla ex.
  • 1½ c all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 c rolled oats
  • ½ tsp salt (opt.)
  • 1 c raisins if you're weird
steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until creamed
  3. Add eggs & vanilla; mix well
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt; add and mix well
  5. Add oats (and raisins); mix well
  6. Using a cookie scoop or rounded spoon, drop
  7. Bake 8-10 mins or until light golden brown.
